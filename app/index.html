About

We are HackHarvard, the oldest student organization at Harvard College interested in promoting technology entrepreneurship on campus. We work hard to create a community of hundreds of students passionate about designing, building, and programming.

HackHarvard brings together CS students from all four years to share ideas, help each other code, and form friendships. To accomplish our goals, the club holds weekly HackNights, dinners, and mixers that give the Harvard community an opportunity to work together on personal projects and get to know each other. We also welcome speakers and organize a variety of tech-related events throughout the year.`
